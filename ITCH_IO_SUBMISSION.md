## Long Description

```markdown
# GlideAudio

GlideAudio is a focused desktop app for cleaning spoken audio in video and audio files without dragging a simple fix through a full DAW. Load a file, analyze the signal, tune the cleanup, preview the difference, and export either a cleaned audio file or a repaired video.

## Why People Use GlideAudio

- Clean noisy spoken audio quickly
- Improve tutorial, talking-head, and podcast-style voice tracks
- Keep media local instead of uploading source files to a cloud editor

## Key Features

- Noise reduction
- Voice clarity enhancement
- De-echo and de-hum controls
- Loudness leveling and limiter
- Export cleaned audio or repaired video

## Great For

- Tutorials and screen recordings
- Talking-head videos
- Podcast and creator workflows
```
