# GlideAudio Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-07

GlideAudio is a local-first Windows desktop app for cleaning spoken audio in audio and video files.

## What GlideAudio Collects

GlideAudio does not require an account.

GlideAudio does not intentionally collect:

- personal profile information
- cloud-hosted media uploads
- analytics or usage telemetry
- advertising identifiers

## How GlideAudio Processes Media

GlideAudio processes media on the local device.

The app uses local tools such as FFmpeg and FFprobe to:

- inspect media metadata
- analyze audio levels and noise characteristics
- render preview loops
- export cleaned audio or repaired video files

Your source media stays on the device unless you choose to copy, move, or upload it yourself outside the app.

## Files GlideAudio Creates

GlideAudio may create:

- temporary preview audio files while the app is running
- exported output files in the folder you choose
- a local settings file to remember session preferences between launches

The settings file stores practical app state such as preset, preview length, loudness target, export mode, export format, and output folder.

## Network Use

The core GlideAudio workflow does not require internet access.

If you open external links, download FFmpeg separately, or use third-party distribution platforms, those services may have their own privacy practices.

## Third-Party Components

GlideAudio depends on local software libraries and may be bundled with FFmpeg binaries for media processing. Those components operate locally as part of the app workflow.

## Data Retention

GlideAudio keeps exported files and saved settings on the local device until you delete them.

Temporary preview files are intended to be cleaned up by the app when they are no longer needed.

## Children

GlideAudio is a general-purpose desktop utility and is not directed specifically to children.

## Changes

If the privacy behavior of GlideAudio changes materially, this policy should be updated before release.

Support and privacy contact details should be added before public distribution.
